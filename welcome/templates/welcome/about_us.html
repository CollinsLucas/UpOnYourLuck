The Idea

Up On Your Luck assists those who are facing homelessness by providing a platform to share their story and receive donations from those willing to give.

The Team

Up On Your Luck was created as a final project by a team of the following students at Wake Technical Community College in Raleigh, North Carolina.

Despite being a school project, Up On Your Luck is a serious undertaking.
