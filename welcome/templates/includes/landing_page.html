What Does This Service Do?

Creates a profile page for a person who is homeless that displays their life story and donation links.

In addition, a user will be sent a QR-coded sticker which can be placed on a cardboard sign.

When a passerby scans the User's QR-coded sticker, they will be redirected to the User's profile page where they can read about their life and or donate.
